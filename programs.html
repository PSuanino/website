Programs Offered

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Our flagship program designed to prepare students for careers in software development, artificial intelligence, data science, and cybersecurity.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Curriculum includes courses in programming languages, algorithms, database management, and more.
  • Hands-on projects and internships with industry partners.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Explore the intersection of technology and business with our IT program. Gain skills in network administration, web development, and IT project management.

  • Duration: 4 years
  • Learn to design and maintain IT infrastructure for organizations.
  • Opportunities for certifications such as CompTIA, Cisco, and Microsoft.

Master of Science in Computer Engineering

For students seeking advanced knowledge in hardware and software integration, embedded systems, and robotics.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Advanced coursework in digital systems design, microprocessors, and machine learning.
  • Thesis research and industry collaborations.
